SIGCITE 2025 Tutorial

Weaponizing Words:

Direct & Indirect Prompt Injection Attacks on LLM

AI systems don’t break like software; they fail in silence, misclassify with confidence, and hallucinate under pressure. This 75-minute hands-on workshop exposes the core vulnerabilities of modern AI with LLM manipulation. You will actively craft exploits. The focus is practical: how do these attacks work, how can you launch them, and what can actually stop them?

About the Instructor

Pavan Reddy

Pavan Reddy is an AI security researcher and the founder of Adversarial Lab, an open-source framework for red-teaming machine learning models. His work focuses on adversarial attacks, prompt injection vulnerabilities, and the systemic weaknesses of foundation models. He’s presented research at venues like HCI International and FLAIRS, and has given talks at conferences like SquadCon and PyTexas Meetups and educates a wide audience online, where over 90,000 followers tune in for his insights on AI risk and resilience.
